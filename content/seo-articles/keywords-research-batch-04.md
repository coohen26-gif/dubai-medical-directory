# DMD — Long-Tail Keyword Research (Batch 04)

## Methodology

Long-tail keywords selected against 4 criteria:
1. **Multilingual intent** (the directory targets FR/AR/EN/RU/ZH expats)
2. **Local transactional signal** (Dubai + neighbourhood OR Dubai + specialty)
3. **Low-medium competition** (long-tail = 3+ words, no big aggregator ranking 1)
4. **DHA-licensed service** (we can fulfill the intent with our dataset)

Estimated volume uses Google Keyword Planner ranges; competition scored 0-100 (lower = easier).
